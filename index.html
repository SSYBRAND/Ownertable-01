How its start...?

SSY Brand is a company and organization founded by three 11th-grade students, Satyam  ,Saumya and Shivank The company's goal is to create innovative tech items, military equipment, weapons, and automobiles. At present, FulsomeX publishes blogs on tech, science, and engineering. Aseemam was founded on Aug 01, 2023, in Prayagraj, India. The company's headquarters is located in a small apartment that the two founders share. FulsomeX is a bootstrapped company, meaning that the founders have funded the company themselves. The company's name, Aseemam, is a combination of the words "Aseemamhelp" and "Org" Aseemam means "abundant" or "copious," and Org represents the company's potential to achieve great things. Aseemam mission is to "create innovative products that make a positive impact on the world." The company's vision is to be "a leading provider of tech, military, and automotive products." Aseemam is still in its early stages, but the company has big dreams. The founders are passionate about technology and engineering, and they are determined to make a difference in the world.
